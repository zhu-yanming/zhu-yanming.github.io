  This work provides a novel framework for recovering high-quality 3D Gaussian Splatting representations from low-resolution multi-view captures. By integrating 2D super-resolution priors with cross-view geometric consistency, it supports more robust 3D reconstruction for real-world rendering, virtual reality, and immersive visualization applications.
