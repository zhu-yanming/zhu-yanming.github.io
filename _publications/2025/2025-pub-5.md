abstract: >- 
  This work develops ViewCloud as a lightweight 3D representation for practical 3D recognition and cross-domain retrieval. By combining the efficiency of point clouds with the semantic richness of multi-view renderings, it supports low-cost, scalable, and deployment-friendly 3D understanding for CAD, robotics, and real-world visual retrieval applications.
