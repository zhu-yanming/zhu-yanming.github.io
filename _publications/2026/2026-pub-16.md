abstract: >- 
  This work develops a 3D-aware generative framework for controllable gaze synthesis. It models gaze through explicit eyeball rotation in canonical 3D space, enabling diverse image generation with physically consistent eye motion and reliable gaze labels. Its few-shot adaptation strategy further transfers target-domain appearance from a small number of unlabeled images, producing synthetic data that consistently improve downstream gaze estimation.
