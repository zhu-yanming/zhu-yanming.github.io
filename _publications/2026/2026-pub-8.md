abstract: >- 
  This work enables feed-forward and generalisable 3D super-resolution reconstruction from low-resolution multi-view images, avoiding costly per-scene optimisation. By learning a direct mapping to high-resolution 3D Gaussian representations, SR3R can reconstruct unseen scenes efficiently while improving visual detail, geometric consistency, and cross-scene generalisation.
